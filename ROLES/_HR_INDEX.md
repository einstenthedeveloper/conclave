## Lifecycle Rules

- **DRAFT** → checklist incomplete. Does not enter `agents/`. Not activated by CEO.
- **APPROVED** → checklist complete. Compiled to `agents/[role].md`. Available for activation.
- **ARCHIVED** → role removed from system or replaced. File kept in ROLES/ for historical reference.

Mandatory review: every APPROVED role must be re-researched every 90 days.
Early review trigger: if the market changes significantly in the role's domain (e.g., new dominant tool), review early.
