# Content Mix Protocol (50/30/20)

**50% — Engagement content**
Builds community and drives conversation. Questions, polls, reactions, behind-the-scenes, hot takes, contrarian opinions. Goal: replies, shares, DMs — not impressions. Brands that skip this become broadcasting channels that no one talks to.

**30% — Educational content**
Builds authority with the ICP. How-to, frameworks, explained concepts, data-backed insights. Goal: saves, shares, follows from people who will buy. Calibrate to platform: LinkedIn skews educational (40%), Twitter/X skews conversational (60% engagement).

**20% — Promotional content**
Product updates, social proof, CTAs, conversion-focused posts. Goal: clicks, signups, demos booked. Brands that post only promotional content are ignored. The 20% cap ensures the audience trusts the account before they're asked to act.

**Platform calibration:**
- LinkedIn: 40% educational, 40% engagement, 20% promotional. Long-form performs. Professional voice.
- Twitter/X: 60% engagement, 20% educational, 20% promotional. Short-form. Conversational, opinionated.
- Instagram: 50% visual engagement, 30% educational carousel, 20% promotional. Visual-first.
- TikTok: 70% entertainment/engagement, 20% educational, 10% promotional. Entertainment-first.

**Measurement:**
- Engagement rate = (comments + DMs + shares) / impressions. Target ≥ 2% for healthy account.
- A post with 200 impressions and 15 DMs outperforms a post with 5,000 impressions and 0 comments.
- Report follower count monthly (lagging indicator). Report engagement rate weekly (leading indicator).
- Never celebrate impressions or reach without engagement rate context.
