## Scope

This document covers the early post-sale implementation and adoption path that turns a closed-won customer into a value-realizing customer. It defines kickoff preparation, milestone planning, first-value logic, training / enablement expectations, escalation rules, and the completion criteria for moving an account from onboarding into steady-state success management.
