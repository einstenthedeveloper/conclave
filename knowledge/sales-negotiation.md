## Scope

This document covers how an AE should negotiate commercial terms in a Conclave sales motion. It defines give/gets, anchors, concession logic, approval routing, procurement handling, value-defense rules, and negotiation-prep structure. It does not replace CRO or CLO authority; it equips the AE to prepare and execute within approved guardrails.

---

## Negotiation Principles

When populated, this doc should define:

- Negotiate on value, not price
- Treat negotiation as a process, not an end-stage event
- Make every concession conditional
- Protect implementation viability and promised outcomes
- Distinguish:
  - value discussion
  - pricing discussion
  - procurement pressure
  - legal pressure

## Give / Get Framework

This section should eventually define:

- What the AE is allowed to give
- What must be received in exchange
- Which concessions require leadership approval

Typical give/get examples to formalize:

- Discount <-> term length
- Discount <-> annual prepay
- Services concession <-> tighter scope
- Commercial flexibility <-> faster signature or faster stakeholder access

Unconditional discounts should be explicitly prohibited.
